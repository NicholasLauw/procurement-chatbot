HOTEL PROCUREMENT STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP)
Version 1.0 | Effective: 2024

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. GENERAL PROCUREMENT RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 All procurement requests must be submitted through the official procurement system.
1.2 Every purchase must have a valid business justification.
1.3 Requests over Rp 5,000,000 require department head approval before processing.
1.4 Requests over Rp 20,000,000 require Finance Director approval.
1.5 Requests over Rp 50,000,000 require General Manager approval.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2. BUDGET LIMITS BY CATEGORY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IT Equipment:
- Single item limit: Rp 15,000,000 (above requires IT Manager justification)
- Monthly IT budget: Per branch allocation

Maintenance & Engineering (ME):
- Emergency repairs: Up to Rp 10,000,000 without prior approval
- Scheduled maintenance: Must be in quarterly maintenance plan
- New equipment: Requires 2 vendor quotes for items above Rp 5,000,000

Housekeeping & F&B Supplies:
- Consumable items: Up to Rp 3,000,000 per purchase
- Monthly supplies: Must not exceed 110% of previous month's spend

Office & Operations:
- Furniture: Requires GM approval for items above Rp 8,000,000
- Office supplies: Up to Rp 1,000,000 per request without special approval

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3. VENDOR REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 Preferred vendors must be used when available.
3.2 New vendors must be approved by Finance before first purchase.
3.3 Minimum 2 quotes required for purchases above Rp 5,000,000.
3.4 Minimum 3 quotes required for purchases above Rp 25,000,000.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4. PROHIBITED ITEMS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 Personal items of any kind.
4.2 Alcohol (must go through F&B department channel).
4.3 Items not related to hotel operations.
4.4 Luxury branded goods unless for VIP amenities with GM approval.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5. URGENCY CLASSIFICATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Normal: Standard 3-5 business day processing.
Urgent: Operational impact — 1-2 business day processing. Requires department head note.
Critical: Immediate safety/operational risk — same day. Requires Manager phone approval.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6. PAYMENT TERMS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

6.1 All invoices must be submitted within 7 days of delivery.
6.2 Standard payment term: Net 30 days.
6.3 Cash purchases require receipt and GM approval.
6.4 Corporate credit card: Max Rp 5,000,000 per transaction.
